Our Mission

Our mission is to develop a user-friendly data-driven platform that calculates carbon emissions based on everyday activities such as electricity consumption, transport usage, and fuel consumption. The system provides clear analytics and eco-friendly suggestions that help users reduce their environmental impact. By combining data analysis and web technologies, CarbonSense provides users with insightful information about how their lifestyle contributes to carbon emissions. The mission is also to encourage responsible energy usage and promote sustainable habits in daily life.

Our Vision

Our vision is to promote environmental awareness by helping individuals understand their carbon footprint and encouraging responsible and sustainable lifestyle choices. CarbonSense aims to contribute to a greener future by making carbon emission analysis simple, accessible, and understandable for everyone. We believe that technology can play an important role in supporting environmental protection and sustainability initiatives. Our platform aims to inspire people to take small but meaningful actions that collectively reduce global carbon emissions.

Our Goals

The goal of CarbonSense is to create awareness about climate change and empower people to make informed decisions that reduce carbon emissions. Through simple calculations, visual analytics, and sustainability suggestions, the project aims to support environmentally responsible living. Another goal is to demonstrate how modern web technologies and data analytics can be used to solve real-world environmental challenges. The project also aims to educate users about sustainable practices that can contribute to a healthier planet
